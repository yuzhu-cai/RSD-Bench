About Music Collaborator

Music Collaborator is a web application designed to facilitate music collaboration among users. It allows users to create, share, and edit music projects seamlessly.

Features include:

  • User registration and login
  • Create and manage music projects
  • Collaborate with other users
  • Upload and share music files
