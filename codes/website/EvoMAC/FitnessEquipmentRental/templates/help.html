Help Page

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • How do I rent equipment?
    To rent equipment, navigate to the Equipment page, select the equipment you want, and fill out the rental form.
  • What payment methods are accepted?
    We accept various payment methods, including credit cards and PayPal.
  • How can I return rented equipment?
    You can return rented equipment by navigating to the My Rentals page and selecting the return option.

Contact Support

If you need further assistance, please contact our support team at support@fitnessequipmentrental.com.

Accessibility Information

We are committed to ensuring accessibility for all users. If you require assistance or have any accessibility-related questions, please reach out to our support team.
